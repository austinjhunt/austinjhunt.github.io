How it works

Three taps from photo to listing.

1

Snap or pick

Open the Capture tab, take a photo of your item, or choose one from your library.

2

Add context (optional)

Type a quick note — model name, brand, included accessories. Gemini uses it to sharpen the draft.

3

Generate & share

Get a draft listing in seconds. Edit anything, copy fields individually, and open Marketplace or eBay to paste.
